Introduction

About us

We are a Finnish company specializing in professional car care. We offer a wide range of services, including detailing, painting, wheel restoration, ceramic coatings, window tinting, leather repair, film wrapping, and much more. Our mission is to provide the highest quality services that not only improve the appearance of your car, but also extend its service life.

Years of experience

We have serviced more than 65,000 cars of various makes and models.

Modern equipment

We use only proven, high-quality materials from leading Finnish manufacturers.

Professional team

Our experts are regularly trained and certified in the latest car care techniques.

Individual approach

Each car receives individual service according to its specific characteristics and customer needs.

Our approach to quality:

Detailed diagnosis:

Before starting work, we conduct a detailed inspection of the vehicle to determine the optimal set of services.

Quality control:

Each stage of the work undergoes strict control to guarantee a flawless result.

Feedback:

We value our customers' opinions and are constantly improving our services based on the feedback we receive. We also make calls every two weeks to gather feedback from our partners.

Benefits

Advantages of cooperation:

Increased vehicle liquidity

Professional cleaning improves the appearance, makes the car look more expensive, and speeds up the sale. Detailing helps hide minor flaws and restore the car's shine and freshness.

Additional income

Offer detailing services to buyers as an option — for example, “premium preparation” or “ceramic coating before winter.”

You can:

  • include services in the cost of the car;
  • sell separately as an upsell;
  • receive a percentage of the resale of our packages.

Delegation without infrastructure costs

Offer detailing services to buyers as an option — for example, Instead of your own car wash or detailing area, you hand over the tasks to professionals.

  • No need for employees, equipment, or cleaning products;
  • No quality control costs — we guarantee the result;
  • Flexible format: we come to you or work at our base.

Increased customer confidence

A clean, well-maintained car inspires more confidence in the buyer. This is especially important when selling a used car or after a test drive — aesthetics directly influence the purchase decision.
